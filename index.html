The Not So Negative Approach

Life can be a hassle at times. It has its ups and downs, pros and cons etc… But many a times we fail to see the true essence of what life really is. Too frequently we give ourselves to the devastating things that happen around us. We let our demons control what and who we really are. We hold ourselves a slave to our assumptions and conclusions. And in the end…what do we get out of it?
